## 2026-03-09 - Visual Scannability in Reports
**Learning:** In text-heavy compliance reports (like SSP), visual anchors (emojis, progress bars) significantly improve scannability and high-level comprehension without breaking data contracts.
**Action:** Use helper functions like `get_status_emoji` and `get_progress_bar` to consistently inject visual metadata into human-readable outputs (Markdown/PDF).
