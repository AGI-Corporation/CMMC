## 2026-05-22 - [Visual Progress and Status Indicators in Markdown Reports]
**Learning:** Adding visual cues like emojis, star-based confidence meters, and ASCII progress bars to dense Markdown reports significantly improves scannability and user experience without requiring a complex UI.
**Action:** Use `get_status_emoji`, `get_confidence_stars`, and `get_progress_bar` patterns when generating human-readable compliance documents to provide immediate visual feedback.
