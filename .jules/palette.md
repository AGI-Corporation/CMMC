## 2025-05-15 - Visual Scannability for Compliance Reports
**Learning:** For text-based/Markdown compliance reports, users benefit from visual "micro-status" indicators (emojis, progress bars, star ratings) that allow for rapid scannability without relying solely on reading dense tables or text fields.
**Action:** Use `get_status_emoji`, `get_progress_bar`, and `get_confidence_stars` helper functions when generating human-readable compliance summaries to provide immediate visual context alongside raw data.
