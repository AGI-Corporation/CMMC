## 2026-03-05 - Visual Scannability in Compliance Reports
**Learning:** Visual indicators like emojis, star ratings, and progress bars significantly improve the scannability of text-based compliance reports (SSP). Emojis help identify status at a glance, while star ratings provide a quick intuition of confidence levels that raw percentages lack.
**Action:** When generating text or markdown reports, always include visual cues (emojis, bars, or stars) for key status indicators to help human readers parse information faster.
