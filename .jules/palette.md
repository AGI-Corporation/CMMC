## 2026-03-05 - Visual Progress and Status Indicators in Markdown Reports
**Learning:** For terminal-based or report-driven compliance tools, Markdown-compatible visual indicators (like block characters `█` and Unicode emojis ✅) significantly improve scannability for human reviewers compared to raw text labels.
**Action:** Use `get_status_emoji` and `get_progress_bar` helper patterns when generating Markdown or PDF reports to provide immediate visual context of system readiness.
